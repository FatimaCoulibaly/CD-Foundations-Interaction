5 Albums To Enjoy While In Quarantine

Here are some albums I'm enjoying while in quarantine. #QuarantinePlaylist

Ungodly Hour

Chole x Halle

Chloe x Halle is an American R&B duo composed of sisters Chloe and Halle Bailey. My favorite song from Ungodly Hour is "Forgive Me" and "ROYL."

Last Year Was Weird, Vol 2

Tkay Maidza

Takudzwa Victoria Rosa "Tkay" Maidza is a Zimbabwean-born Australian singer-songwriter and rapper. My favorite song from this album is "You Sad" and "Don't Call Again" ft Kari Faux.

B7

Brandy

Brandy Rayana Norwood, better known by her mononym Brandy, is an American singer, songwriter, record producer, actress and businesswoman. I love the her songs "Rather Be" and "Bye BiPolar"

Dirty Computer

Janelle Monáe

Janelle Monáe Robinson is an American singer, songwriter, rapper, actress, and record producer. Some of her best songs are "Screwed" ft. Zoë Kravitz and "I Got the Juice" ft. Pharrell Williams.

Running To The Sun

St. Beauty

St. Beauty is an American musical duo consisting of singers Alexe Belle and Isis Valentino. I love their songs "Not Discuss It" and "Colors."
